Алфавит! Alphabet!

There are 33 characters (10 vowels, 21 consonants, and 2 'signs'). Here is what they look like! Click on one to hear it's pronunciation.

And an image of the alphabet:

There are some letters that look the same as English and generally sound the same: A – E – K – M – O – T.

There are other letters that look the same as English but sound very different: В – Н – Р – С – У – Х.

Finally, there are letters that look pretty different from English: Б – Г – Д – И – Й – Л – П – Ф – Ц - Ж – З – Ч – Ш – Щ – Ё – Ы – Э – Ю – Я - ъ – ь
